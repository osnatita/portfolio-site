Tax FAQ
Why was my tax form voided? ▾
Tax forms are voided when information changes significantly or when a newer form supersedes the previous one. You'll need to submit a new form to resume payments.
Why do I have withholding on my tax form? ▾
This is likely because you did not provide a verified TIN from your country's tax authorities. Providing a valid TIN can reduce your withholding rate to 0.0%.
What is a W-8BEN form? ▾
A W-8BEN is a US tax form used by non-US individuals to certify their foreign status and claim tax treaty benefits, potentially reducing the amount withheld from payments.
How often do I need to renew? ▾
W-8BEN forms are valid for 3 years. You'll receive a notification before expiration so you can renew without payment interruption.
Can I use W-9 instead of W-8BEN? ▾
W-9 is for US persons only. If you are a non-US individual or entity, you must use the appropriate W-8 series form.

Verification FAQ
Why do I need to verify? ▾
Verification is required to comply with anti-money laundering (AML) regulations and to confirm your identity before releasing payments.
What documents are accepted? ▾
We accept government-issued ID, passports, business registration documents, and utility bills for address verification. Accepted document types vary by country.
How long does verification take? ▾
Most verifications are processed within 1–2 business days. Complex cases may take up to 5 business days.
What happens if verification fails? ▾
You'll see exactly which fields failed and why. You can retake verification with corrected information at any time.

Design Decisions
Wallet Home Alert-first hierarchy: critical compliance issues (paused payments) surface above the fold before any navigation. Notifications are grouped to reduce cognitive load — recipient doesn't have to hunt across sections to understand their status.
Alert hierarchy Status clarity Compliance UX
Context
Trolley Recipient Portal 4M+ recipients across 210+ countries. Core challenge: making dense regulatory requirements (W-8/W-9/DAC7, AML verification, global payout routing) self-serve for non-expert users in varied legal jurisdictions.
My Role
End-to-end ownership of the recipient-facing portal. Led research, interaction design, accessibility audit (WCAG 2.1), and cross-functional delivery with engineering, legal, and compliance teams.
